What is Credit Transfer?

Credit Transfer is a program allowing students to complete a 2.5 or 3-year course in a single examination. Ideal for those who paused their studies, it grants the opportunity to fast-track education. Upon successful completion, the student is awarded a recognized degree, enhancing career prospects and academic credentials.

Eligibility Criteria for Credit Transfer Degree

  • Suitable for students with breaks in their academic years.
  • Must have completed initial years (1st/2nd) in the same discipline.
  • Essential documents: hall ticket, enrollment number, previous marksheets.
  • Earn your higher degree via one consolidated examination.
  • Last completed year of examination is considered for credit mapping.
  • Flexibility: Opt for classroom or distance mode, as per convenience.
  • Direct entry into the next educational phase post your last cleared stage.

Benefits of Credit Transfer Degree

  • Fast-track degree completion, saving time and effort.
  • Career advancement with promotions and higher designations.
  • Bridge academic gaps without repeating the entire course.
  • Flexible learning: No daily attendance required, suitable for working professionals.
  • Enhanced resume and credibility in the job market.
  • Globally recognized credentials, accepted for higher studies.
  • Approved by various embassies, enabling international opportunities.
  • Re-ignite your academic journey and achieve your educational goals swiftly.

How It Works

1. Assessment

Our team evaluates your previous academic records and identifies eligible credits for transfer.

2. Enrollment

Complete the enrollment form, submit required documents, and choose your preferred mode of study.

3. Preparation

Access study materials online, attend optional classes (if chosen), and prepare for the single examination.

4. Examination

Sit for one consolidated exam, testing all course competencies. Clear the exam, earn your degree.
